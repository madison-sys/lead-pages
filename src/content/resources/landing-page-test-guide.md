## What's Inside

This guide walks through the complete landing page launch process — from the decisions you need to make before building, to the QA checklist that catches the errors no one tells you about.

**What you'll learn:**
- **One audience, one CTA** — why pages built for everyone convert no one, and how to write the one sentence that focuses everything
- **The copy framework** — headline, subheadline, value props, and CTA text in a structure that works before you have data
- **Form optimization rules** — every extra field drops conversion 11%; here's what to keep and what to cut
- **Pre-launch QA** — the 9-point checklist covering speed, mobile, tracking, and form submission before sending a single click

## Who This Is For

Anyone building a lead magnet or opt-in page who wants to get it right the first time — not after spending $500 in ad spend figuring out why nothing converts.

This is especially useful if you've built pages before that "looked good" but underperformed, or if you're launching a new resource and want a repeatable launch process you can run in under an hour.
